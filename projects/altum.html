Altum Inc.

A brief summary of my time with a startup making an AI product for content writing.

Overview

Client: Altum, Inc. (Startup)

Nov. 2019 – Oct. 2020

Summary

  • Altum sells AI tools to SEO marketers through its SaaS product, Jenni AI.
  • As the sole UX designer I took on multiple roles to work with Business and Development.
  • I learned about the benefits and rewards of making compromises that keep a product afloat in the market.

Roles

  • UX Designer, UI Designer, User Researcher, and Everything Else
  • Wore multiple hats to create a pleasant user experience for customers.
  • Worked extensively with Business and Development to direct product growth and address usability concerns.

Who is Altum?

Altum is a small startup that ran Jenni AI, a content writing service. Its mission is “... to usher in a new era of human creativity through artificial intelligence”. Our 4-person team included Business, Development, and Design.

Who are Altum's users?

Jenni’s clients include SEO marketers and marketing agencies who needed:

  • SEO tools and services that helped their own content outrank their competitors’.
  • Reliable and affordable writers that produced high quality content they didn’t need to edit.
  • Information that would help them outrank their competitors.

What I did at Altum

My main role was improving the user experience for customers by designing for Jenni’s UI.

As part of the overall team, I followed Altum’s product development process. We used a hybrid of Lean and Agile methodologies where we worked in two-week sprints and kept track of tasks done within sprints using a kanban board.

Improving Altum

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic Altum pivoted from a content writing service business focus into a SaaS business model.

Finding solutions

We found that giving both long-term and short-term design strategies equal weight not only helped the business stay afloat but grew it too.

Why Altum exists

At the end of the day, Altum is a place where like minded individuals who love to learn, grow, and build meaningful things gather to create extraordinary things together.
