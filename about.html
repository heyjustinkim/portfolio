My design philosophy

A design with context is a design that knows what it is and who it helps. Take Altum's design process:

When people ask why we only looped between defining problems and ideating solutions I tell them it's because as a startup we didn't have the time and resources. But if I never mention the context of the process then they wouldn't understand.

To quote a wise mentor of mine, "Great design starts with why you're doing it in the first place." In other words, context.
